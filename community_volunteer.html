-VOLUNTEER-
various charities and organizations with branches located in the Bay Area

MedShare
2937 Alvarado St, San Leandro, CA 94577
This humanitarian aid organization collects and distributes surplus medical supplies to over 100 countries. Bay Area volunteers can support MedShare at its Western Region Distribution Center.
One Warm Coat
Find a drive near you!
Just as its name describes, this organization hosts coat drive events nationwide. Individuals can donate their coats at drives near their location or even start their own.
Open Heart Kitchen
5200 Case Ave, Pleasanton, CA
Serving the Tri-Valley area (Dublin, Pleasanton, and Livermore), Open Heart Kitchen provides healthy meals for anyone who needs them. Anyone over ten years old is welcome to volunteer for this amazing non-profit.
Rise Against Hunger
2296 Tripaldi Way Hayward, CA 94545
This international organization provides food and aid to those who need it most. Volunteers in the Bay Area can package meals and perform other tasks to do their part to end world hunger. They also serve at various locations in Dublin, Pleasanton, and Livermore.
